About

Language expert.
AI insider.

I am a Tanzanian copywriter and AI Content Trainer who specialises in the gap between what AI produces and what African audiences actually respond to. Native Swahili speaker. Professional English writer. Two years inside AI training pipelines at Outlier and Alignerr, evaluating and improving how large language models handle tone, context, and cultural nuance in one of the world's most underserved language groups.

What I bring to AI companies: Swahili-English language data and evaluation for LLM training pipelines. I read AI responses the way a native speaker does, not the way a rubric does. When a model gets the tone wrong or misses something culturally, I can say exactly why and document it in a way other reviewers can use.

What I bring to brands: Copy in English and Swahili that moves people toward a decision. Landing pages, email sequences, product descriptions, and web content. I write for East African audiences because I am one.

I write copy that converts. I train AI to do the same.
