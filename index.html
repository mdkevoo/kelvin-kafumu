About
Language expert.

I'm Kelvin Kafumu, a copywriter, AI content trainer, and Swahili language specialist based in Dar es Salaam, Tanzania.

My work sits at a rare intersection: native Swahili fluency + deep NLP expertise. While most AI systems were trained on Western languages, I've spent the last two years actively fixing that by engineering prompts, building evaluation sets, and teaching large language models how Swahili actually works: its noun classes, morphology, dialectal variation, and cultural nuance.

Before AI, I spent years as a professional translator and content writer, handling legal documents, marketing copy, and technical manuals. That foundation in precision and clarity is what makes my AI training work actually effective.

My positioning is simple: "I write. I train AI to write." Both halves matter.
